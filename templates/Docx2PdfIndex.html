Document Converter

Welcome to the Document Converter Tool. This tool allows you to upload various types of documents and convert them to PDF.

Supported formats: .docx, .doc, .pdf, .html

Please follow these steps:

  1. Select a document file from your device using the "Choose File" button below.
  2. Click the "Convert & Upload" button to begin the conversion process.
  3. Once the conversion is complete, you'll see a download link for the converted PDF file.
